A work environment promoting innovation should also be a place that allows personal initiatives to take shape. Whether that is to learn about entrepreneurship, dive deeper into a computer domain or raise voices for a cause, 42 prides itself in its culture of associations and support.

Our values

A Human Formation

42 understands that each person is unique. Diversity is a strength both during training and on the labor market. The training supports exchange, rewards sharing, and advocates concern for others. The school adapts to the pace of each individual. Through the teachings received at 42, students develop their social skills to become more than just experts in their fields.
